## test 1 — stand still and watch it wobble

face the hotspot. hold phone steady ~1 min. dont move.

every 15 sec jot down the degree number and how full the alignment bar looks (rough % is fine):

- 0s: ___°  bar ~___%  
- 15s: ___°  bar ~___%  
- 30s: ___°  bar ~___%  
- 45s: ___°  bar ~___%  
- 60s: ___°  bar ~___%  

**good:** bar stays mostly full if youre still pointing at it. degrees might twitch a few degrees thats ok  
**bad:** bar drops to empty while youre not moving. degrees jumping 30+ for no reason. haptics stop
